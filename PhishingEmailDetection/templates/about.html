ℹ️ About PhishGuard

Defending your inbox with heuristic analysis and machine learning.

Project Overview

PhishGuard is a tool designed to analyze .eml files and detect phishing attempts using a powerful combination of heuristics and machine learning. We use the extensive PhishStorm dataset to train or fine-tune our lightweight ML models.

We provide a working, desktop-responsive Flask application. By processing sample emails, PhishGuard calculates an aggregate risk score, highlights triggered security rules, and provides an AI-driven explanation panel to help users understand why an email is flagged.

How It Works Under the Hood

Our detection engine relies on a two-pronged approach to ensure accuracy and speed:

  • Heuristics: Before the AI even touches the text, we use heuristic rules and regular expressions to instantly flag suspicious patterns, such as urgent keywords ("action required", "locked out"), mismatched sender domains, and hidden URLs.
  • AI Prompting & Roles: When an email snippet is sent to the AI, we use prompt engineering to assign the model a specific "role" (e.g., instructing it to act as an expert cybersecurity analyst). This focuses the model's output on threat detection.
  • Token Management: Raw emails contain massive amounts of header data and HTML. We carefully extract the plain text and manage the token limits before sending it to our local models, ensuring we get fast, accurate responses without exceeding the AI's context window.

Deployment Roadmap

Currently, PhishGuard runs entirely on localhost. This local-first approach ensures maximum privacy, as sensitive email files are processed directly on your machine without being sent to external third-party cloud APIs.

As the project evolves, our goal is to containerize the application and deploy it as a fully hosted, public website, making these advanced phishing detection tools accessible to anyone from any browser.
