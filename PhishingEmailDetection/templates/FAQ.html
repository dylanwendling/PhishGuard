❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about how PhishGuard protects your inbox.

What is PhishGuard?

PhishGuard is an AI-powered tool designed to analyze emails for potential phishing threats. It evaluates different email features and provides a risk score with explanations.

How does PhishGuard work?

PhishGuard uses machine learning models such as SmolLM2-135M, Llama-3.2-1B, and Qwen2.5-1.5B trained on phishing and legitimate email datasets. It analyzes sender information, email content, links, and attachments to estimate phishing risk.

Is PhishGuard free to use?

Yes, PhishGuard is currently free to use and is intended to help users stay protected from phishing attacks.

How accurate is PhishGuard?

No phishing detector can guarantee 100% accuracy. PhishGuard aims to provide strong detection support, but users should still be cautious with suspicious emails.

What do I do if the message looks suspicious?

Do not click links or download attachments. Report the email to your IT team or administrator and avoid interacting with the sender.

How do you detect phishing emails?

PhishGuard checks sender identity, suspicious links, message wording, and attachments. It then uses machine learning to identify patterns often found in phishing emails.

What is a breach and where does the data come from?

A breach is an unauthorized exposure of sensitive data. PhishGuard’s training data may come from public phishing datasets and reported phishing examples used for research and model improvement.
